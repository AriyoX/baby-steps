# Privacy Policy for Baby Steps

**Last updated:** April 16, 2026

Baby Steps ("we," "our," or "us") is an educational app for young children and their parents/guardians. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When a parent/guardian creates an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Password (handled through Supabase Authentication)

### Child Profile Information
When adding a child profile, we collect parent-provided information such as:
- Child name
- Child gender
- Child age/range
- Learning focus/reason (onboarding responses)

### Learning and Progress Information
To power the learning experience and parent dashboard, we process:
- Game/activity progress and completion data
- Activity metadata (for example: activity type, activity name, score, duration, completion time, and details)
- Achievement/unlock data
- Weekly learning summaries and usage stats

### Local Device Storage
The app stores certain data locally on the device (AsyncStorage), including:
- Authentication session data
- Language preference
- Onboarding status
- Locally cached progress/session information

### Optional Media Access
In coloring features, if you choose to save artwork, the app may request media library/photo access to:
- Save images to your device gallery (for example, a "ColoringBook" album)
- Share images using your device sharing tools

### Text Sent to Third-Party Language Services
If text-to-speech or translation features are used, text content may be sent to Sunbird AI API endpoints to generate translated text or audio.

## 2. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect
Based on the current app implementation, we do not intentionally collect:
- Camera recordings (camera permission is blocked)
- Microphone recordings (record audio permission is blocked)
- Precise location data
- Contacts
- In-app advertising identifiers for ad targeting

## 3. How We Use Information
We use collected information to:
- Create and maintain parent accounts
- Create/manage child profiles
- Personalize educational content and learning flow
- Track learning progress and achievements
- Enable parent dashboard insights
- Provide password reset and account security features
- Operate translation/text-to-speech features when requested
- Improve app reliability and user experience

## 4. How We Share Information
We may share information with service providers that help operate the app:
- **Supabase** (authentication and database storage)
- **Sunbird AI** (language translation/text-to-speech processing, when those features are used)

We may also share information:
- When you explicitly choose to share content using your device sharing features
- If required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security

We do not sell personal information.

## 5. Data Retention
We retain data for as long as needed to provide the app and related services, including:
- Account and profile data while your account remains active
- Learning/progress records needed for parent dashboards and continuity
- Local device data until it is removed, app storage is cleared, or the app is uninstalled

Saved images remain in your device gallery until you delete them.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Baby Steps is intended for children’s learning, but account control is designed for parents/guardians. Parents/guardians are responsible for:
- Providing profile information for children
- Supervising a child’s use of the app
- Requesting updates or deletion of child-related data

## 7. Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we work to reduce risks and protect user data.

## 8. International Data Processing
Depending on your region and our providers’ infrastructure, data may be processed or stored in countries outside your own.

## 9. Your Choices and Rights
You can:
- Update profile details in the app where available
- Manage optional media permissions through your device settings
- Stop use of translation/text-to-speech features if you do not want text sent to third-party language services
- Request account or data deletion by contacting us

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made.

## 11. Contact Us
For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact:
- **Email:** hello@babystepslearn.com
- **Organization/App Team:** Baby Steps
