Baby Steps - Account and Data Deletion Requests

Developer/App Team: Baby Steps

Last updated: April 16, 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of their Baby Steps account and associated data, or request deletion of specific data without deleting their account.

Request account deletion (and associated data)

  1. Email hello@babystepslearn.com with subject: Delete My Baby Steps Account.
  2. From your registered account email, include:
    • Your Baby Steps account email address
    • Optional: child profile name(s) to help us identify your records faster
  3. We verify the request and process deletion.

Request deletion of some data without deleting your account

  1. Email hello@babystepslearn.com with subject: Delete Specific Baby Steps Data.
  2. Tell us which data you want deleted, for example:
    • One or more child profiles
    • Activity history
    • Achievement/progress history
  3. We verify the request and process the selected deletion.

What is deleted, what may be retained, and for how long

When you request account deletion, we delete:

  • Account record (authentication account linked to your email)
  • Child profile data linked to your account (such as name, gender, age range, and learning focus)
  • Associated app data linked to your account (such as activities, achievements, and progress records)

We may retain limited data for a short period:

  • Security, fraud-prevention, legal compliance, or dispute-resolution records, where required
  • Backup copies for disaster recovery

Retention timeline:

  • Primary account/data deletion target: within 30 days of verified request
  • Backup retention: up to 90 days before automatic overwrite/removal
