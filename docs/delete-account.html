Baby Steps Account and Data Deletion

Developer/App Team: Baby Steps

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This page explains how parents can request deletion of a Baby Steps account and associated user-owned data.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open Baby Steps and sign in to the parent account.
  2. Go to Settings, then Account, then Delete Account.
  3. Read what will happen during the 30-day deletion period.
  4. Type DELETE and choose Schedule Account Deletion.

If you cannot access the app

Contact Baby Steps support at hello@babystepslearn.com and ask to delete your account. If possible, email us from the address used for your Baby Steps account.

What happens after you request deletion

  • Your Baby Steps account is scheduled for deletion.
  • Your child profiles and learning progress are hidden during the deletion period.
  • You are signed out of the app.
  • Shared Baby Steps learning content is not deleted because it is not owned by one family account.

30-day reactivation period

You have 30 days to change your mind. During this period, you can log in again and reactivate your account. Reactivating restores your child profiles and learning progress where available.

After the deletion period ends

After 30 days, the account is no longer available for reactivation and is prepared for final removal by Baby Steps' secure removal process. The app will not say that final deletion is complete until that process has finished.

Some limited information may be kept only if needed for legal, security, fraud-prevention, dispute, compliance, backup, or operational reasons.

Data deleted or anonymized

After the deletion period, Baby Steps will delete or anonymize associated user-owned data, such as:

  • Parent account information connected to the Baby Steps account
  • Child profile information provided by the parent
  • Child learning progress, activity history, and achievement history tied to the account
  • Account-specific app data that Baby Steps no longer needs to provide the service

Data not deleted because it is shared app content

Shared learning content, language content, stories, achievement definitions, and global Baby Steps app content are not user-owned and are not deleted when a family account is deleted.

Contact support

For account deletion, data deletion, or privacy help, contact hello@babystepslearn.com.
